沖縄地方

OKINAWA REGION

独自の歴史と文化を持ち、温暖な気候と美しい海に囲まれた沖縄地方。伝統的には泡盛が主流でしたが、近年ではワイン造りも始まっています！リュウキュウガネブという沖縄原産の山葡萄を用いたワイン生産が始まり、地域の新たな魅力として期待されています。

The Okinawa region has a unique history and culture, and is surrounded by a mild climate and beautiful sea. Traditionally, Awamori has been the mainstay of the region, but in recent years wine production has also begun! Wine production using Ryukyu Ganebu, a mountain grape native to Okinawa, has begun and is expected to become a new attraction for the region.

恩納村

ONNASON

美しい海の近くのオーベルジュで
沖縄初醸造のワインが飲めるかも？！

沖縄本島中部に位置する恩納村は、リゾート地として知られ、美しい海と自然が魅力。近年、オーベルジュのオーナーである夫妻が、リュウキュウガネブの栽培からワイン醸造までを手掛けています。オーベルジュでの食事も要チェック。

Enjoy wine near the beautiful sea at an auberge
You might try the first wines produced in Okinawa!

Onnason, located in central Okinawa, is known as a resort area with beautiful sea and natural attractions. Recently, a couple who owns an auberge has started cultivating and producing wine from the native Ryukyu Ganebu grape. Be sure to try the meals at the auberge as well.
